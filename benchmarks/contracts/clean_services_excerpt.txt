SERVICES AGREEMENT

1. Payment. Customer shall pay Provider undisputed fees within thirty days after receiving a valid invoice. Customer may dispute an invoice in good faith by written notice within ten days.

2. Termination. Either party may terminate this Agreement for material breach after thirty days' written notice and an opportunity to cure. Either party may terminate for convenience on sixty days' written notice.

3. Confidentiality. Each party shall protect confidential and proprietary information using reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards and may use it only to perform this Agreement.

4. Assignment. Neither party may assign this Agreement without the other party's prior written consent, except to an affiliate or successor in connection with a merger or sale of substantially all assets.

5. Governing Law. This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of New York, without regard to conflict of law principles. The parties consent to courts located in New York County, New York.
